## 🧭 What this app does

guardrailed-ai-prospector helps you create and manage B2B cold email outreach with AI. It uses multiple AI agents to draft, review, and improve sales emails before they go out.

It is built for users who want a structured way to handle outreach without writing every message by hand. The app uses guardrails to keep the output more consistent and safer to use.

This setup helps you move from a rough idea to a finished email faster.

## 📧 Cold email workflow

A simple workflow looks like this:

1. Add a prospect or company name.
2. Choose your offer or service.
3. Let the AI draft the first message.
4. Review the subject line and body.
5. Check for tone, spelling, and clarity.
6. Test the email before sending.
7. Send the final version through your email setup.

This keeps your outreach process steady and easier to manage.
